The barista handed Silvia a bunch of napkins and gave her a free drink out of sympathy, saying “Some people will do anything for the coffee fix.”

Olivia was not having it that day, and she promptly stepped back in front of Vera. Vera turned to Olivia and gave her a very mean look. Olivia stared back at Vera, as though daring her with her eyes to do something.Olivia refusing to engage with Vera, turned to the barista and said, ‘I was first in line!” Vera saw a small cup of cream on the counter, picked it up, and splashed it in Olivia’s face. Everyone in line gasped. Olivia stood there gaping, cream dripping from her chin. The barista ran out from behind the counter and ordered Vera to leave. Vera stared at Olivia one last time, then stormed out of the coffee shop, yelling, “I hate coffee!” Everyone at the coffee shop turned to each other wondering if that had really just happened. The barista handed Olivia a bunch of napkins and gave her a free drink out of sympathy, saying “The nectar of the gods makes people do crazy things.”

Ben was astonished and said, “Excuse me?!’ Nick ignored him and proceeded to tell the barista that he would like a coffee. Ben stared back at Nick, as though daring him with his eyes to do something.Ben refusing to engage with Nick, turned to the barista and said, ‘I was first in line!” Nick saw a small cup of cream on the counter, picked it up, and splashed it in Ben’s face. Everyone in line gasped. Ben stood there gaping, cream dripping from his chin. The barista called over to a policeman standing in line, and he ordered Nick to leave before anyone pressed charges. Nick stared at Ben one last time, then stormed out of the coffee shop, yelling, “I hate coffee!” Everyone at the coffee shop turned to each other wondering if that had really just happened. The barista handed Ben a bunch of napkins and gave him a free drink out of sympathy, saying “I think some caffeine would’ve helped that one.”
